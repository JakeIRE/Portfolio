in commandprompt compile and run l4Hotel e.g:
'javac l4Hotel.java'
'java l4Hotel'

To navigate through the menus simply enter a number when prompted with options, the corresponding number will be displayed on the left of the options

When using staff or supervisor usernames and passwords are stored in the corresponding text files, a sample username is 'me' and password is '123' for both staff and supervisors

at any point hit q to exit
